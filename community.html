Recipe of the Month

Grilled Peach and Burrata Salad

Ingredients

  1. 4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
  2. 8 ounces burrata cheese
  3. 2 cups arugula
  4. 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  5. 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  6. Salt and pepper to taste
  7. Optional: fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the cut side of the peaches with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the peaches on the grill, cut side down, and cook for 3-4 minutes until grill marks form.
  4. Flip the peaches and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until softened.
  5. Remove the peaches from the grill and let them cool slightly.
  6. Arrange the arugula on a serving platter.
  7. Tear the burrata cheese into pieces and scatter them over the arugula.
  8. Place the grilled peaches on top of the burrata.
  9. Drizzle the salad with balsamic glaze and a drizzle of olive oil.
  10. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  12. Serve immediately and enjoy this delicious summer salad!

This Grilled Peach and Burrata Salad is the perfect combination of sweet and savory flavors, making it a refreshing and elegant dish for any occasion.
